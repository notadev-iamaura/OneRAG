# AR-200 Product Specification

The AR-200 is an autonomous warehouse robot built by Acme Robotics.

- Payload capacity: 50 kg
- Maximum speed: 2 m/s
- Battery life: 8 hours on a single charge
- Charging time: 90 minutes to full capacity
- Operating temperature range: 0 to 40 degrees Celsius
- Onboard sensors: LIDAR, 6 cameras, and 12 proximity sensors
- Firmware version at launch was 3.2.1, released on 2026-01-10.
- The warranty period is 24 months from the date of delivery.
